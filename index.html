About Us

Connecting Bulgaria’s Industries with Quality Blue-Collar Talent from Asia

Navon Jobs Bulgaria, a branch office of the globally trusted Navon Jobs, specializes in recruiting and bringing skilled blue-collar workers from Asia to Bulgaria. With expertise across multiple industries, we connect Bulgarian businesses with dedicated, skilled professionals, ensuring a reliable, long-term workforce to meet your company’s needs.

Navon Jobs Bulgaria is the Bulgarian branch of the Navon Jobs brand, a global leader in workforce recruitment. Specializing in bringing skilled blue-collar workers from various Asian countries to Bulgaria, we help Bulgarian businesses meet the demands of their industries by providing dependable, professional labor.

Our recruitment expertise covers multiple sectors, including manufacturing, construction, agriculture, hospitality, logistics, and more. As an organization, we are committed to ethical recruitment practices, quality assurance, and supporting a seamless integration of international talent into the Bulgarian workforce.

Our Recruitment Process

Streamlined, Efficient, and Tailored to Meet Your Business Needs

Step 1: Needs Assessment

We begin by understanding your workforce needs, specific job requirements, and company culture to ensure we find the right candidates.

Step 2: Candidate Sourcing and Screening

Using our extensive network and rigorous screening process, we identify skilled candidates with the right qualifications, experience, and work ethic.

Step 3: Documentation & Compliance

Our team handles all documentation, including work permits, visas, and contracts, ensuring full compliance with Bulgarian labor laws and immigration policies.

Step 4: Pre-Departure Orientation

To facilitate a smooth transition, we provide candidates with cultural orientation and basic language skills, preparing them to integrate seamlessly into the Bulgarian workplace.

Step 5: Arrival and Onboarding

Once candidates arrive in Bulgaria, we assist with initial settling-in, providing any necessary support to help them adjust to their new roles.

Step 6: Ongoing Support

Navon Jobs Bulgaria maintains regular contact with both employers and employees to address any concerns, support retention, and foster positive, productive employment relationships.

Emilia Foundation

Our Story

We strongly believe that giving enriches you, and anyone can offer a smile, a kind word, love, compassion, or understanding – a piece of your heart! Every day we receive gifts that we should appreciate: a new day of life, a new sunrise, the clear blue sky, the beautiful nature that delights our eyes and lifts our spirits, and the pure smile of a child. Let us give, by loving, and love, by giving! Only in this way will we understand the good that a simple smile can bring.

If you want to advance on the path of knowledge, gift a book; if you want to have an enlightened heart, offer comfort to those in need; if you want to know freedom, buy a dove from the market and set it free to fly towards the sky.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring prosperity to families and the Neamț community, to support young people in discovering their vocations through educational, cultural, and artistic programs, and to help those in need lead a decent life, because the well-being of each individual depends on the common good.

Our Vision

In our vision, every person can contribute to a better world with a smile or a kind word. We believe that only through mutual support can we have a united community based on healthy values, and only then can we live in peace and harmony.

Our Values

Our values: generosity, respect, involvement, assistance, Christianity, altruism, gratitude, generosity.

Our Goal

Our goal is to initiate social programs and cultural and educational events to create a favorable environment for the development of children and families from disadvantaged backgrounds.

The Emilia Foundation operates in the localities of Neamț County, which has over 446,000 inhabitants. Many families in the county live on the edge of existence, children have difficult access to education and other extracurricular school programs.
