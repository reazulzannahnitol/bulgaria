Founder's Passion

“Welcome to Navon Jobs Bulgaria!

With a forward-looking vision and a deep commitment to creating value for both Bulgarian employers and Asian employees, Navon Jobs Bulgaria is more than just a recruitment agency – we are a trusted partner. This initiative began with a mission to build lasting connections between companies and skilled individuals, contributing to economic growth and the development of a dedicated workforce.

We understand that, in today’s world, talent and dedication know no boundaries. That’s why we work tirelessly to bridge cultural gaps, streamline the recruitment process, and support both employers and employees every step of the way. Our aim is not only to meet your workforce needs but to exceed expectations by delivering a seamless, ethical, and reliable recruitment experience.

Thank you for considering Navon Jobs Bulgaria. I look forward to supporting your business and welcoming skilled professionals to a new chapter in their careers.”

Warm regards,
Georgiana Mart
CEO, Navon Jobs
